Maluma Fan Page

Bio

Juan Luis Londoño was born on January 28, 1994 in Medellin, Colombia. The name Maluma was created at 15 years old when his was his first record was being produced ("No quiero")- his name is comprised of the first 2 letters of his mother, father, and older sister's names (Marlli Arias, Luis Londoño, Manuela Luis Londoño). Maluma is a well known singer, songwriter, rapper, and actor signed to Sony Music Colombia, Sony Latin and Fox Music.
